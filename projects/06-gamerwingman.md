# GamerWingman — Deep Dive Report

> **Category:** Consumer App (Gaming)  
> **Status:** 🔶 WIP / Prototype  
> **Monetization:** ✅ Google Play Billing ($4.99/mo)  
> **Est. Y1 Revenue:** $24K–$120K

---

## Overview
AI gaming companion Android app — tracks playtime across Steam/Xbox/PSN/Epic, AI avatar with Ready Player Me + Mixamo animations, squad matchmaking, mood tracking, voice commands ("Hey Wingman"), playstyle analysis via local Ollama LLM.

## Tech Stack
- **Engine:** Unity 6 LTS (C#)
- **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions)
- **AI/ML:** Ollama (llama3.2/phi-3), Porcupine wake word
- **3D:** Ready Player Me avatars, Mixamo animations
- **Audio:** ElevenLabs TTS
- **Payments:** Google Play Billing
